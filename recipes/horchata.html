Horchata

What is Horchata?

This Authentic Mexican drink, horchata, is a cool, creamy and refreshing drink. Horchata is often described as a sweet rice milk beverage. It's a simple drink but very delicious. You can find this drink in almost any Mexican resturant.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • cinnamon stick
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1.5 cups of milk or almond milk
  • 1 liter of water
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Start by soaking the rice, cinnamon, and almonds in a bowl of water over night or at least for 5 hours.
  2. Strain the water from the cinnamon, rice, and almond mixture that were soaking, dispose of water.
  3. Blend the cinnamon, rice, and almond mixture with the evaporated milk until smooth.
  4. Strain the blended mixture into a pitcher. Add the sugar, vanilla, and milk.
  5. Mix well until everything is mixed. Add the liter of water and ice.
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
