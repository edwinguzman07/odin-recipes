Quesibirrias

What are Quesibirrias?

Quesibirrias is a Mexican originated dish from Tijuana, Mexico. This delicious dish is made from cooked beef that is folded into a corn tortilla with melted cheese and top with chopped cilantro, diced onion, and your favorite salsa of your choice (salsa roja or verde).

Ingredients

  • 700g beef chuck
  • 2 dried ancho chilies
  • 2 dried guajillo chilies
  • 1 dried chipotle chilies
  • 1 tbsp of cloves ground
  • 1 tbsp of black ground pepper
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp of majoram
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1/2 tsp of cumin
  • 1/2 tsp of ground ginger
  • salt
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon sick
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • boilder water
  • 2 cups of beef stock
  • vegetable oil
  • corn tortillas
  • diced onions
  • oaxaca cheese
  • cilantro

Instructions

Preparing the beef

  1. Preheat oven at 350Â°F.
  2. Start de-seeding the ancho, guajillo, and chipotle chilies.
  3. Soak the chilies in 2 cups of boiling water for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Chop the beef chuck into medium-sized pieces. Season them with salt and pepper.
  5. Grab a dutch oven, add oil and sear beef pieces on each side for 5-7 minutes. until brown on each side.
  6. Pour 2 cups of water into the beef, salt to taste, bring to a boil and cook beef on medium heat fo 30 mintues. (This creates the stock for the consome.)

Making the sauce

  1. Add the chilies and the water that soaked into a blender
  2. Add apple cider vinegar, tomatoes, onion, garlic cloves, cloves, marjoram, thyme, oregano, cumin, ginger, salt to taste and black pepper to the blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Pour the sauce into the dutch oven with beef.
  4. Add the bay leaves, cinnamon stick, salt to taste and the black pepper to the water if it becomes too dry.
  5. Once the beef is ready. Remove the beef from the oven and use a fork to shred it.

Making the Quesibirra Tacos

  1. Add a few drops of oil to a pan.
  2. Dip the corn tortilla in the consome.
  3. Add the tortilla to the pan, add the shredded beef to half of the tortilla, pour a bit more consome over the tortilla, the flip the taco and cook on the other side for a few mintues.
  4. Remove the taco from the pan and top the taco with diced onions, oaxaca cheese, and cilantro.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
